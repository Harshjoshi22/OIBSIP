Biography

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an illustrious Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. Born in 1931, Dr. Kalam played a pivotal role in India’s missile development and space programs, earning him the title “Missile Man of India.”

Beyond his scientific achievements, Kalam was known for his humility, vision for India’s future, and dedication to inspiring youth and education across the nation. His presidency (2002–2007) was marked by efforts to elevate India’s global stature through science, technology, and innovation.

Dr. Kalam's legacy continues as a symbol of perseverance, knowledge, and service.
